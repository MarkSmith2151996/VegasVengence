The Destination

Where Speed
Meets the Strip

Vegas Vengeance is the world's first adults-only speed and automotive-themed entertainment resort. Set just minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, the park combines world-class thrill rides, a full-scale casino, premium nightlife, and chef-driven dining into one high-octane destination.

Aventador Escape

Our flagship hypercoaster and the tallest in the Western United States. A Lamborghini-inspired launch coaster that goes from 0 to 140 mph in 3.8 seconds, climbing 560 feet before a near-vertical drop into a subterranean tunnel.
